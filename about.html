About Us

Immerse yourself in the serenity of nature and craft everlasting memories with your loved ones at Kubukiran Farm Resort. Nestled in the heart of Brgy. Janagdong, Mogpog, Marinduque, our haven was founded in 2022 to offer a unique escape from the ordinary.

At Kubukiran, we go beyond the typical beach getaway, providing both tourists and locals with an alternative experience that embraces the charm of provincial living. Our location boasts clean, invigorating air and breathtaking landscapes that paint the perfect backdrop for a rejuvenating retreat.

Whether you choose a private rental for a Day or Night Stay or opt for the tranquility of an Overnight Stay, Kabukiran Home and Rental Cafe is your ideal haven. Create lasting memories as you bask in the beauty of nature and relish the distinctive charm of our farm resort. Escape the mundane and discover the enchantment that awaits you at Kubukiran.
