Kabukiran Home and Rental FAQ

Location

1. Where is the location of Kabukiran Home and Rental?
Brgy. Janagdong, Mogpog, Marinduque.

Check-in and Check-out

2. What are the check-in and check-out times?
Day Stay: 9:00 am to 4:00 pm
Night Stay: 5:00 pm to 11:30 pm
Overnight Stay: 5:00 pm to 9:00 am

Parking

3. Is there parking available on-site?
Yes, Kabukiran has designated parking space.

Food and Beverages

4. Can I bring outside food and beverages to the pool area?
Yes, you can bring outside food and beverages; however, please avoid loitering.

Pets

5. Are pets allowed?
Pets are welcome; just keep them in control.

Smoking

6. Is smoking allowed on the premises?
Smoking is allowed; please avoid littering.

Amenities

7. What amenities are included with the rental?
In day or night stay: Use of adult pool, Use of big tent, 2 Outdoor umbrellas, 1 Small kubo, 1 Big Kubo.
In overnight stay: Exclusive use of adult pool and the whole place, Overnight stay in kubo (5:00 pm-9:00 am), Use of bathtub, Electric kettle, Electric fan, Bathrobe, and Breakfast (optional).

Pool Maintenance

8. How is the pool maintained and cleaned?
The pool is regularly maintained by cleaning the pool's filters and pumps to ensure proper circulation and sanitation.

Age Restrictions

9. Are there any age restrictions for guests using the pool?
All ages are welcome.

Extending Stay

10. What is the process for extending my stay?
To extend your stay, please make an early booking to avoid overlapping reservations with other guests.

Minimum Stay Requirement

11. Is there a minimum stay requirement?
No, there is no minimum stay requirement as guests have the option to rent the entire property.
